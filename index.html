Help Guide

1. How to Add New Complaint
  • Log in to your account
  • On the home screen, tap on Add Complaint or Request tab
  • Navigate to the Complaints section
  • Get photos/videos or you can skip.
  • Fill in the complaint details (e.g., title, description).
  • Submit the form. You will receive a reference number for tracking.
2. How to Add a New Request
  • Log in to your account.
  • On the home screen, tap on Add Complaint or Request tab.
  • Navigate to the Requests section.
  • Fill in the request details (e.g., title, description).
  • If you want, you can attach a file, but it's not compulsory.
  • Submit the form. You will receive a reference number for tracking.
3. Review the Latest Complaint or Request
  • Log in to your account.
  • Go to the home screen, tap on Latest Complaint/Request section.
  • Click the Details button.
  • View the status and details of the complaint.
4. Complaint and Request History View
  • Log in to your account.
  • Navigate to the History section.
  • View a list of all your past complaints and requests.
  • Tap on any entry to see detailed information and status.
